Exploring Baltimore City Salaries with D3

The data displayed below is public Baltimore City salaries from the 2015 fiscal year, which spans from July 1, 2014 through June 30, 2015. The raw source data can be found at the baltimore open data portal

Scatterplot Matrix

Scatterplot matrices help to discover correlations and groupings between large numbers of variables. Here we have displayed the matrix of gross-pay, annual salary, and hire date for Baltimore positions.

Clicking and dragging in any of the subplots will select just a subset of the data to plot in all figures. Refreshing the browser will reset this selection.
